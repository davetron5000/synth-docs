Features

Chord / Strum Mode

  1. Hold Shift and press Hold. Chord will become orange and Hold will blink

  2. Rotate 1 / Type to choose the chord

  3. Hit single keys on the keyboard to trigger that chord

  4. Rotate 2 / Notes to add more notes to the chord

  5. Rotate 3 / Vel > Notes to adjust how many notes are played based on velocity:

    Value # of Notes
    0% All notes no matter what
    50% About 1⁄2 at low velocity, all at high
    99% Only the root at low velocity, all at high

  6. Rotate 4 / Strum to strum the values instead of playing chords

The more notes in your chord, the more this affect is noticeable

Control Mode / Using the Knobs

  1. By default, 1 / Type, 2 / Notes, 3 / Vel > Notes, and 4 / Strum will control the first bank of MIDI CC's you've configured in MIDI Control Center

  2. Tap Chord to advance to Bank 2

  3. Repeat to advance to Banks 3 and then 4

When Chord is orange, you are in Chord Mode, and the knobs don't send CCs

Setting a User Chord

  1. Hold down Chord and play a chord

  2. You should now be in Chord Mode and can play your chord with one note
