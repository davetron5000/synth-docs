Copy Entire Pattern

  1. Select the pattern to copy from

  1. Press Copy

  3. Optionally, press Bank and then a target bank

Mute Instruments / Parts

  1. Tap Mute

  2. Tap the instrument/part to mute or unmute

  3. Tapping Mute again will unmute everything

  4. Tapping Mute again re-mute what was muted previously

  5. Hold Shift and tap Mute to clear all solo'ed instruments from the group

This is the behavior when in Toggle mode, which is set in MIDI Control Center

Polyrhythms / Step Count Per Instrument

  1. Hold Shift and tap 16 to enable Polyrhythms for the pattern (if needed)

  2. Tap one of the pads to configure its pattern length

  3. Tap Step to enter step edit mode

  4. Hold Last Step and tap one of the buttons 1‚Äì16 to set the last step for that instrument

Random for Instrument

  1. Tap the instrument/part to edit (button 1‚Äì6)

  2. Tap Current Track, next to the Random knob.

  3. Adjust Random to add random behavior to that track.

Random for Pattern

  1. If lit, tap Current Track, next to the Random knob so that it's unlit.

  2. Adjust Random to add random behavior to the pattern.

Ratchet or Flam Steps

  1. Tap the instrument to edit

  2. Hold down a step button 1‚Äì16

  3. Tap the Roller to set the repeat:

    • 1⁄4 for 1 trigger
    • 1⁄8 for 2 triggers
    • 1⁄16 for 3 triggers
    • 1⁄32 for 4 triggers

When holding down the step, the light above the Roller indicates the current state

Solo Instruments / Parts

  1. Tap Solo

  2. Tap the instrument/part to solo or remove from solo group

  3. Tapping Solo again will unmute everything

  4. Tapping Solo again re-solo what was solo'ed previously

  5. Hold Shift and tap Solo to clear all solo'ed instruments from the group

This is the behavior when in Toggle mode, which is set in MIDI Control Center

Notes and Non-Obvious Behaviors

  • You must hit Sync to select MIDI if using MIDI. Every. Single. Time.

  • Closed HH will choke Open HH

  • When cable is plugged into individual out, it's removed from the main out and headphones out

  • Distortion only applies to instruments sent into Main out, not anything on individual outs
