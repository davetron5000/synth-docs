Features

Cycling Envelope WTF

  1. The Mode controls what the Cycling Envelope does:

    Value Behavior
    Env Acts as a single envelope
    Run Acts as a free-running LFO
    Loop Acts as an LFO that is triggered and restarted by Gate/Key events

  2. Hold Shift and turn Rise to adjust the shape of attack

  3. Hold Shift and turn Fall to adjust the shape of decay

Modulating Modulation

  1. Hold down one of the Assign buttons

  2. Turn the Matrix to select the modulation point to modulation

  3. Release the Assign button

Modulating Unison

  1. Hold down one of the Assign buttons

  2. Press the Paraphonic button

  2. Hold down Shift, then rotate Type to bring up the sample menu

Unison

  1. Hold down Shift and hold down Paraphonic

  2. Release Shift

  3. Rotate Preset to adjust unison detune

  4. Pressing Paraphonic will disable unison

Make sure your preset is saved before doing this, just in case you mess it up
